What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Typically, our retreat encourages the involvement of the entire company, but we can also work with various group sizes from corporate participation, individual departments, and even smaller business groups. The selected events will help those attending to resolve any tension between team members, improve motivation, and create a stronger company wide bond.

What size teams can you accommodate?

Unplugged retreat accommodates large corporate groups down to 15 people or less. With our ability to engage varying group sizes, we can organize events to accommodate your needs. From twice a year, scheduled events can last from two to three days to weeklong offsite events. Our retreat is the perfect place to build bonds between the entire company to individual departments and even smaller group participation.

Is there really no wifi available?

For those times when you absolutely cannot continue without contacting someone, we have a dedicated computer room and very fast internet contection with wi-fi access.
